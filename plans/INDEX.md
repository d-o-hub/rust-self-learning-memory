## Naming Conventions

Documents follow these naming conventions:

- `UPPERCASE.md` - Primary documents and reports
- `CATEGORY/document.md` - Organized by category (STATUS/, ROADMAPS/, etc.)
- `FEATURE_NAME.md` - Feature-specific documents
- `YYYY-MM-DD` suffix - Date-specific reports
- `PHASE#_` prefix - Phase-specific planning documents
